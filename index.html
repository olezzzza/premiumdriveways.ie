What We Do

Expert Driveway Solutions

We specialise exclusively in driveways. One focus, done perfectly. Every project is tailored to your home and budget.

Most Popular
✨

Resin Bound Driveway

Two-component polymer resin mixed with aggregate. Seamless, permeable, UV-stable. 40+ colours. Lasts 20+ years with minimal maintenance. SUDS compliant.

🧱

Block Paving (6cm)

Heavy-duty 60mm concrete blocks rated for car traffic. Classic look, easy to repair, adds significant property value. Available in many colours and patterns.

⚫

Tarmac / Asphalt

Most cost-effective and fastest to install. Smooth, durable black surface. Ideal for larger driveways. Ready to use within 24 hours of laying.

🏗️

Concrete Driveway

Plain poured concrete — clean, modern and extremely durable. Lasts 30+ years. Brushed or exposed aggregate finish. Best value long-term option.

💪

Reinforced Concrete

Steel rebar or mesh reinforced concrete for maximum strength. Ideal for heavy vehicles, vans or areas with soft ground. Engineered to last a lifetime.

🪨

Gravel / Crushed Stone

Natural, affordable and quick to install. Excellent drainage. Ideal for long rural driveways. Various stone sizes and colours. Easy to top up over time.

🌿

Grass Grid / Eco Pavers

Hollow interlocking pavers filled with grass or gravel. Green, permeable and planning-friendly. Blends with the garden while supporting vehicle weight.

🛣️

Large Concrete Slabs

Precast slabs (up to 2m x 1.5m). Fast installation, industrial strength. Popular for gated entrances and wide driveways. Clean, minimal aesthetic.

🚗

Tyre Tracks / Wheel Strips

Two parallel strips (concrete, block or resin) with grass or gravel between. Budget-friendly, low maintenance and visually appealing. Great for long drives.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The cost depends on size, material and site conditions. As a guide: resin bound driveways start from €60/m², block paving from €50/m², and tarmac from €35/m². A typical family home driveway ranges from €3,000 to €12,000. Contact us for an accurate free quote tailored to your project.
Most residential driveways are completed in 2–5 days. Resin bound surfaces cure within 24 hours. Block paving for a standard home takes 3–5 days. We'll give you an exact timeline in your quote.
In most cases, no planning permission is required provided you use permeable materials (like resin bound) or include adequate drainage. Our team handles all technical requirements and ensures full compliance with local regulations.
Resin bound is our top recommendation for the Irish climate — it handles freeze-thaw cycles well, won't crack, drains rainwater efficiently and stays weed-free. Block paving is also excellent for durability. We'll recommend the best option for your specific situation during your free consultation.
We cover all of East Ireland: Dublin, Wicklow, Wexford, Kildare, Meath, Louth, Carlow, Kilkenny and Waterford. If you're unsure, just contact us and we'll confirm.
Yes — every driveway we install comes with a 10-year workmanship guarantee. If any issue arises due to our workmanship within that period, we will return and rectify it at no cost to you.
